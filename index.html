Finding some beauty in the madness...

This is pretty much the reason why I write. ☺ When I feel anxious or out of place, my hand immediately itches to pick up a pencil and just write. To me, it is a way to express the feelings and jot down the words I was unable to physically utter. This is why I created chlover's letters, a passion project which combines my love for coding and writing in order to create a safe place for me to write down my rambles :)

Why do I paint? Art to me has always represented a place to unleash my emotions and a place for me to unwind. It has been a shelter of mine, providing a safe environment wheran express myself.

Ever since I was a little girl, I always remember doodling on any surface in front of me. A napkin, a piece of paper I could have otherwise discarded.

The point being, art has always been used, whether as an outlet of expression, a weapon of war, or a tool which allowed a person or peoples to speak up when their words and voices would have otherwise been stifled.

I also am very interested in the context of art- I love reading about the impact artistic practices have had throughout time and space in impacting humankind, for better or for worse, depending on the cirumstances..

Just like umy artistic practice however, I do not paint my lines with the intent of being a muse, a pedestal upon which to be admired.

Similarly, the source of where I get my artistic inspiration can not have much context at times. Sometimes, I just feel down and aimlessly start doodling a sketch for the sake of it.

Other times, I pick up my pen with more intent, determined on a set outcome. Regardless, art has always been the vessel through which I express my thoughts and feelings when I thought they could never rise to the surface.
